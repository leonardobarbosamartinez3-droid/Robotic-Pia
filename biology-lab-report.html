Introduction

A cell is the smallest independently functioning unit of a living organism... (summary of intro)

Hypothesis

If we observe different body systems, then we can better understand how the human body works.

Objective

The objective of this practice is to identify, distinguish and investigate the differences between the various organic tissues and sexual related compounds...

  • Controlled: Microscopes
  • Dependent: Type of biological sample
  • Independent: Microscopic characteristics observed

Materials

  • Microscopes
  • Sample of bone marrow
  • Sample of bone
  • Sample of brain
  • Sample of epididymis
  • Sample of sperm
  • Sample of kidney
  • Sample of liver
  • Sample of small intestine
  • Sample of smooth muscle
  • Sample of cardiac muscle
  • Sample of skeletal muscle
  • Sample of epithelial cells and lymphocytes

Methods

We used optical microscopes with 12 prepared histological slides... analyzed and took pictures of each slide.
